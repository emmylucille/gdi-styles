Stacie became enamored with programming when she was waiting for some reporting data and realized that instead of waiting, she could do it for herself! Her first love was SQL and has since done hardware support, SQL reporting, web development (front & back-end) and is constantly learning new things and honing her craft. She is a late bloomer to programming, but has always loved the freedom and power that comes with learning a new language. She loves hey look I am a test link and believes that anyone can learn programming if they just have the desire and a little grit. She will feel like her job is done when we live in a world where no girl decides not to pursue a degree or job in computer science because it's too hard. Stacie also loves game nights with friends, lazy days with her family, and hiking in the gorgeous Utah mountains.
